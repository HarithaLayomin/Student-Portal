Program Overview

Advanced Masterclass in Academic English Communication & Presentation Skills

Mode: 100% Online | Duration: 2 Months | Course Fee: LKR 12,000/- (Payable in two installments)

About the Program

This advanced masterclass is designed for students, undergraduates, and professionals who wish to improve their academic English communication, presentation delivery, and confidence in high-pressure situations such as interviews, vivas, and formal presentations.

The program focuses on practical skill-building, real-world speaking situations, and continuous improvement through guided practice.

Course Syllabus (Key Areas)

  • Presentation & Delivery: Structuring presentations, designing simple yet powerful slides, speaking confidently without over-reliance on notes.
  • Vocal & Physical Communication: Voice control (tone, pace, volume), effective body language and eye contact, using appropriate humor.
  • Confident Academic Communication: Speaking in lectures, seminars, and group discussions, presenting academic ideas with clarity and conviction.
  • Handling High-Pressure Situations: Managing vivas, interviews, and challenging questions, remaining calm and articulate under pressure.
  • Interpersonal Communication: Identifying personal communication styles, adapting communication for different audiences.
  • Team Collaboration & Leadership: Effective teamwork communication, facilitating discussions and delegating tasks.
