About the Program

This advanced masterclass is designed for students, undergraduates, and professionals who wish to improve their academic English communication, presentation delivery, and confidence in high-pressure situations such as interviews, vivas, and formal presentations. The program focuses on practical skill-building, real-world speaking situations, and continuous improvement through guided practice.

Course Syllabus (Key Areas)

Presentation & Delivery

Structuring presentations, designing powerful slides, speaking confidently without notes.

Vocal & Physical Communication

Voice control, effective body language, eye contact, using appropriate humor.

Confident Academic Communication

Speaking in lectures, seminars, group discussions, presenting academic ideas with clarity.

Handling High-Pressure Situations

Managing vivas, interviews, challenging questions, remaining calm and articulate.

Interpersonal Communication

Identifying personal communication styles, adapting communication for different audiences.

Team Collaboration & Leadership

Effective teamwork communication, facilitating discussions, delegating tasks.
